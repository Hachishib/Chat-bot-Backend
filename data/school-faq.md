## About the School

SPIST stands for Southern Philippines Institute of Science and Technology.
It is a private educational institution located in Imus, Cavite, Philippines.
The school offers Pre-school, Basic Education (Kinder to Grade 10), Senior High School (Grade 11 and 12), and College
The main campus is located at Tia Maria Bldg., E. Aguinaldo Highway, Anabu 2-A, Imus, Philippines, 4103.

## Enrollment Requirements

To enroll at SPIST SHS, you need to submit the following:

- Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
- Original Grade 10 Report Card (Form 138) or Temporary Report Card from your previous school
- 2 pieces 2x2 recent ID photo
- Certificate of Good Moral Character
- 2 active cellular numbers (preferred to be owned by the student)
- 1 valid email address

Enrollment for SY 2026-2027 is ongoing. Campus is open Mondays to Fridays, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

## Enrollment Steps


1. Visit the campus during enrollment period (Mondays to Fridays, 8AM–5PM)
2. Get an enrollment form from the Registrar's Office
3. Fill out the enrollment form completely
4. Submit all required documents listed above
5. Proceed to the cashier for payment (if applicable)
6. Receive your registration confirmation and school ID details

For faster processing, make sure all documents are complete before visiting.

## Grading System

SPIST follows the official DepEd grading system for Senior High School:

- Written Work: 25%
- Performance Tasks: 50%
- Quarterly Assessment: 25%

The passing grade is 75. Grades below 75 in any subject may require remedial classes.
Final grades are computed from the four quarterly grades averaged together.

## Examination Schedule

SPIST follows the DepEd quarterly examination system.
Exams are held four times a year, once at the end of each quarter.

Quarter 1 exam: around August–September
Quarter 2 exam: around October–November
Quarter 3 exam: around February–March
Quarter 4 exam: around April–May

Exact dates per quarter are announced by class advisers and through the official SPIST Facebook page closer to the exam period. Students are advised to monitor announcements regularly.

For urgent exam-related concerns, contact your class adviser or visit the Registrar's Office.

## Directions to SPIST Anabu Main Campus

SPIST Anabu Main Campus is located along E. Aguinaldo Highway in Anabu 2-A, Imus, Cavite.

By commute from Manila:

The most direct way to get there from Manila is via bus or a combination of rail and bus.

  Public Bus: You can take buses labeled "Dasmariñas," "Tagaytay," or "Mendez" from terminals like the Parañaque Integrated Terminal Exchange (PITX), Lawton (Plaza Lawton), or Pasay (near MRT-3 Taft Avenue Station). These buses travel along Emilio Aguinaldo Highway. Just ask the conductor to drop you off at "Anabu Kostal" or "Tia Maria Bldg." in Anabu 2-A.

  Van / UV Express: UV Express vans bound for Dasmariñas or Molino often pass through or near this area. You can find these at terminals in Starmall Alabang, Pasay, or Lawton.

  LRT-1 + Bus: You can take the LRT-1 to Baclaran Station or EDSA Station, then board a bus or jeepney heading to Dasmariñas.

By commute from Dasmariñas, Cavite

Coming from the south, the commute is shorter as you are traveling against the typical morning rush-hour flow.

  Public Bus: Any bus heading toward Manila, PITX, or Lawton will pass right in front of the building. Board any of these along Aguinaldo Highway and alight at Anabu 2-A.

  Jeepney: You can take jeepneys with routes like "Dasmariñas - Imus" or "Dasmariñas - Zapote." Look for those that explicitly stay on the highway.
